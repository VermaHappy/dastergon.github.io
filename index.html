Pavlos Ratis

Hi there! I'm Pavlos. I'm a Site Reliability Engineer based in Munich, Germany.

Over time, I worked on a wide range of projects, from writing software to automate and manage multi-server cloud-based infrastructure to developing web applications. I enjoy contributing to open source software and in the past, I contributed to the Gentoo Linux project as an official developer. In addition, I participated in the Google Summer of Code program, twice as a Software Developer, when I was an undergraduate student, and once as a mentor.

I like building software, distributed systems, and expanding my knowledge around the reliability of services and their infrastructure. I have created a few open source SRE projects such as Wheel of Misfortune, to assist teams and individuals get on board the SRE culture. Also, I was the technical reviewer of the "Real World SRE" book from Packt Publishing, where I offered suggestions regarding the content of the book.
