Site Reliability Engineering

A list of various site reliability projects that I have created or contributed over time:
awesome-sre A curated list of Site Reliability Engineering resources.
Wheel of Misfortune Role Playing Game for Incident Management Training.
Availability Calculator Calculate how much downtime should be permitted in your Service Level Objectives.
awesome-chaos-engineering A curated list of Chaos Engineering resources.
postmortem-templates A collection of postmortem templates from various sources.
postmortems.app A postmortem metadata index.
Error Budget Calculator Calculate the error budget of your service.
SRE Workbook - Markdown Templates A collection of templates ported from the SRE Workbook.
Common Disaster Recovery Scenarios A list of common Disaster Recovery (DR) scenarios for software companies.
Cards Against Reliability A fun card game for software engineers and SREs, based on Cards Against Humanity.
